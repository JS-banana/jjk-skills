# AI Vendor Campaign Radar

AI Vendor Campaign Radar is the context for finding actionable AI and developer
opportunities, screening them, and recording validated rows in a Feishu Base.

## Language

**Opportunity**:
A concrete AI or developer activity that a person can enter, claim, submit to,
register for, or be reminded about before it closes.
_Avoid_: News, trend, announcement

**Candidate**:
A raw lead from a search result, aggregator, community post, official page, or
user-forwarded notice before it passes the opportunity gate.
_Avoid_: Record, row, opportunity

**Opportunity Gate**:
The hard acceptance filter that requires actionability, explicit reward, direct
entry or rules URL, cross-checkable source, and open or long-term status.
_Avoid_: Scoring rubric

**Discovery Source**:
A channel that finds possible candidates early, such as AgentDeadlines, search,
community posts, social feeds, or platform listings.
_Avoid_: Proof

**Official Confirmation**:
The official or recognized page that verifies reward, deadline, participation
path, and rules before a candidate can be kept.
_Avoid_: Snippet, repost

**Reminder**:
A previously recorded opportunity whose deadline or status must be checked
before notifying the user.
_Avoid_: New candidate

**Feishu Row**:
The final validated shape written to the campaign Base after an opportunity
passes the gate and matches the live field contract.
_Avoid_: Note, report
